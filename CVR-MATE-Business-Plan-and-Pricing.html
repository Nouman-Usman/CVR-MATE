Our Mission

We are building the intelligent operating system for Nordic sales teams.

CVR-MATE transforms cold registration data from the Danish CVR registry into warm, actionable sales opportunities using state-of-the-art AI.

The Value Prop

  • Speed: Instant company intelligence briefs.
  • Precision: Lead scoring based on financial health.
  • Automation: AI-generated outreach for email and LinkedIn.
